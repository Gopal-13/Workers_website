Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about BuildHire.

Creating a worker profile is easy! Click on "Sign Up" and select "I'm a Worker." Fill in your details, work experience, skills, and upload any certifications. A complete profile helps employers find you more easily.

We offer flexible pricing plans for employers.

You can verify your skills by taking our skills assessment tests. Verified skills appear with a badge on your profile, increasing your credibility with employers.

Safety is our top priority. We recommend all workers complete OSHA safety training (or equivalent), always wear proper PPE, and follow all site-specific safety protocols. Employers can require safety certifications in their job postings.
